Netflix personal trainer is customizable, so you can pick the vibe and choose your coach.

Hear words of motivation.

Need an extra kick in the pants? Choose the Netflix character you want as your coach, then set the threshold for your activity level. Any time you start slacking during a workout, your coach will momentarily mute or pause your music to give you a little pep talk.

Power Netflix with your movement.

Like to sweat it out to your favorite shows while on the elliptical or treadmill? Connect your personal trainer to your tablet or phone via Bluetooth, cue up Netflix, then start your workout. Your show will pause anytime you stop and resume when you do.
